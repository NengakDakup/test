EARN MORE REVENUE STAY IN CONTROL

The Topclick platform is used by publishers of all shapes and sizes to to scale decisions, access multiple ad partners, and perform automated tests proven to increase revenue.

Publishers leverage Topclick to control experiments related to ad testing, mediating ad partners, and improving visitor experiences on their websites.

This allows publishers to plug-in existing ad partners and access thousands of new ones on a single platform.

FEATURES THAT DRIVE REAL GROWTH

Topclick offers an app store filled with additional features that help publishers do things like monetize and convert pages to AMP, adhere to GDPR compliance, manage ads.txt files, test ad placements using machine learning, and much more.

All the apps in the Topclick app store are free and allow publishers to scale decisions that help them grow their site’s traffic, avoid spammy redirects, manage direct ad deals, and much more.

HEADER BIDDING AND MORE…

The Topclick Platform can easily help publishers implement header bidding while using machine learning to optimize partners and avoid many of the common mistakes made with traditional header bidding implementations — such as a singular timeout for all ad units on the page — failing to apply discrepancy rates to bids, etc.
More importantly, Topclick has an easy-to-use interface that allows publishers to do this all themselves; including adding any existing header bidding partners to the Topclick platform.
